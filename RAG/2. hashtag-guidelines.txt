DOCUMENT: Hashtag Guidelines

Hashtag Rules:
- Always use 2-5 hashtags
- Place hashtags at the end of the post
- Avoid generic hashtags like #success or #motivation
- Prefer niche-specific and industry-relevant hashtags

Formatting:
- No more than 5 hashtags
- Do not insert hashtags inside the text
- Keep them clean and aligned with topic

Examples:
#AIInnovation
#GrowthStrategy
#FastTrack
#LeadershipInsights
