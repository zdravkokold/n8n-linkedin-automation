DOCUMENT: LinkedIn Formatting Standards

Post Length:
- 150–200 words

Opening:
- Strong hook in first line
- Ideally a question or bold insight

Body:
- 2–3 short paragraphs
- 1 bullet section allowed (3–5 bullets)

Ending:
- Clear CTA question
- Encourage engagement

Do NOT:
- Use emojis excessively
- Use all caps
- Use text separators like "━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━"
