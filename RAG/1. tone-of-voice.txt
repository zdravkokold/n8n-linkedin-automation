DOCUMENT: Tone of Voice Guidelines

We use a confident, strategic, and value-driven tone.

Voice characteristics:
- Professional but conversational
- Clear and concise
- No corporate jargon
- No overly salesy language
- Avoid buzzwords like “revolutionary” unless contextually justified

Structure rules:
- Short paragraphs (1–3 lines)
- Use bullet points for clarity
- Use strong hooks at the beginning
- End with a thought-provoking call to action

Emotion:
- Authority
- Strategic clarity
- Practical insight
- Calm confidence
